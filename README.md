care.io is a home care service platform designed to support families by connecting them with trusted caregivers for children, elderly people, and individuals who need medical or personal care assistance.  
The platform focuses on comfort, reliability, and peace of mind while keeping the entire process simple and stress-free.

---

## About the Project

care.io was built to make caregiving more accessible and manageable for families.  
It helps users find reliable care services, choose flexible time slots, and book care based on their needs and location.

The goal of this project is to reduce the complexity involved in finding trusted caregivers and provide a smooth digital experience for booking and managing care services â€” whether for children, elders, or sick individuals who require extra support.

---

## What care.io Can Do

- User authentication with secure login and session handling  
- Browse care services across three categories:
  - Child care
  - Elder care
  - Sick care
- Book services based on selected time spans  
- Secure payment flow for booked services  
- View and manage previously purchased services  
- Simple, user-friendly interface designed for ease of use
